## 🔍 Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the application need an internet connection?
The tool provides live price updates from the Indonesia Stock Exchange. It requires a connection to download this fresh information for your analysis.

Does it store my financial data?
All calculations happen on your local computer. No private data leaves your device. You keep control over your analysis.

Can I track multiple stocks?
Yes, you can create a watch list. Manage as many tickers as you need to monitor your portfolio.

How do I clear my search history?
Navigate to the "Settings" menu inside the app. Select "Clear Cache" to reset your local data logs.

What if the prices look old?
Click the "Refresh" button at the top of the interface. This forces the application to query the current market data again.
